DocIQ Sample Company Policy Document

Annual Leave Policy:
- Full-time employees receive 20 days of annual leave per calendar year.
- Carry forward is capped at 5 days.

Sick Leave Policy:
- Employees receive 10 days of paid sick leave per year.
- Medical certificate is required for absences longer than 2 consecutive days.

Work From Home Policy:
- Employees may work from home up to 2 days per week with manager approval.

Travel Reimbursement Policy:
- Flight reimbursement cap for domestic travel is 12000 INR.
- Hotel reimbursement cap is 6000 INR per night.
